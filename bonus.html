The Yellow Adams

The Yellow Adams is a vicious nemesis to FoodMan. Yellow Adams got his name because when he was a kid he really loved bananas. Food Man is a very hungry superhero and his nemesis, the Yellow Adams loves bananas and can digest food that is yellow. He has always been on the way of food man because he would eat up all the yellow foods that food man had gathered.
