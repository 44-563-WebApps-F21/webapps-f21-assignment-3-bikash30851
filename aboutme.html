FoodMan

Food Man is a normal human being. He goes to college and is one of the brightest kids in the class. When we look into his past, his family always suffered from extreme poverty and never had enough to eat. One day he became very frustrated and started to eat some insect off of the ground. The mutated insect that he ate, gave him some power. He developed a stomach that could eat alot of food. This superhero could theoretically eat food enough for 50 men.
